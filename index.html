What is the eosinophil?
The eosinophils are white blood cells specialized in fighting parasites and diseases.
How to calculate absolute eosinophil count?
To calculate absolute eosinophil count you should use the following formula:
absolute eosinophil count = white blood cells * (eosinophils / 100)
